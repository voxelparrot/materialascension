===============================================

This mod does a few things:
Integrates Create's metalworking mechanics with vanilla and modded progression
Brings the vanilla+ weaponry of Basic Weapons to the material variety of More Ores More Gems
Adds advanced handles and rods for better toolmaking and use in modpacks
Includes customizable unique weapons and artifacts using config

Required Mods:
MORE ORES MORE GEMS:  https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/more-ores-more-gems
CREATE: https://modrinth.com/mod/create

Optional Mods:
BETTER COMBAT: https://github.com/ZsoltMolnarrr/BetterCombat
BASIC WEAPONS:  https://github.com/Khazoda/basic-weapons

===============================================
